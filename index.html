Three generations, one family

Our Story

Pakora Junction didn't start as a plan — it grew out of a family that's been feeding Panipat for almost 70 years.

1958

Dawar Sweets is born

Our grandfather opens the very first Dawar Sweets shop in Panipat — the foundation everything else is built on.

2005

A second home opens

Our father builds the second Dawar Sweets branch, carrying the same recipes and the same standards to more of the city.

Today

Pakora Junction by Dawar Sweets & Snacks

Now run by our father and brother, Pakora Junction is the newest extension of the family kitchen — built for snacking, made with the same care as our sweets.

Same family. Same kitchen values. Three generations who'd rather get it right than get it fast.
